Kipochi, Mpesa & Bitcoins

By Michael Kimani

Kipochi was a bitcoin wallet targeted at East Africa, in Kenya – the home of Mpesa. It gained a lot of online traction and bitcoin media coverage! One blog headline ran a title “Bitcoin saves Kenya from its ‘developing’ status!” The bitcoin community, excited as I would have been, took this info and plastered it on every bitcoin in Africa article that came out.

Unfortunately, some of the facts got muddled away along the way. This article we did, for Coinbrief debunks some of the myths surrounding what happened to Kipochi after it got a ton of media coverage. Here’s the full article:
