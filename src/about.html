Africa digital currency association

The Africa Digital Currency Association (ADCA) was conceived in May of 2014 as a non-profit with the objective of informing and educating the public on digital currency technology. It also supports the digital currency community by organizing regular meetups.

Membership is open to individuals or organizations.
